This article examines how Canvas LMS addresses challenges faced by small school districts by consolidating fragmented technology ecosystems. It highlights five advantages: consistent student experiences, deep integrations, improved communication, a global educator community, and AI capabilities that multiply the productivity of smaller IT teams.
