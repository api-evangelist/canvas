As AI becomes ubiquitous in learning environments, higher education's value proposition is shifting from delivering knowledge to developing capability. The author argues education must now focus on teaching learners to evaluate AI outputs, apply knowledge to complex situations, and cultivate distinctly human capabilities that employers increasingly demand.
